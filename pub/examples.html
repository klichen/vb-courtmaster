Volleyball Rotations For Beginners

Below you can see all 6 rotations that all players must go through during a volleyball match. Depending on what position you play, follow a circle (i.e. if you are a setter, follow the setter circle across the six rotations) through each rotation to get comfortable with them and don't forget to apply it during your games!

Review of positions and abbreviations:

S = Setter

P1 = Power hitter 1

P2 = Power hitter 2

M1 = Middle Blocker 1

M2 = Middle Blocker 2

OPP = Opposite Hitter
